GoScale

Web-Scale Cloud Servers Made Easy

GoScale was a platform for elastically scaling and managing cloud servers and services. Part of Y Combinator's Summer 2012 batch, GoScale stopped trading in 2014.

As part of our interview process for Y Combinator, we built iPG - a simulated interview with Paul Graham, which peaked on HackerNews the day of our interview.
